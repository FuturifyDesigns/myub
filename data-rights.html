Data Protection Act, 2024 · Part VIII

Your data rights

Use this page to request access, correction, erasure, restriction, objection, or a copy of your data.

Under Botswana’s Data Protection Act, 2024, you can exercise rights over your personal data. We will verify your identity before fulfilling requests. You may also complain to the Information and Data Protection Commission.

Rights you can exercise

  • Access — get a copy of personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate or incomplete data (many profile fields can be edited in Profile)
  • Erasure — request deletion where the Act allows (also available via Profile → Delete Account)
  • Restriction — ask us to limit processing in certain cases
  • Objection — object to certain processing, including optional presence/push features where applicable
  • Portability — receive data you provided in a commonly used machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent — for processing based on consent (cookies, optional notifications)

Self-service options

  • Profile — update your details or delete your account
  • Cookie Policy — manage cookie preferences
  • Browser / device settings — disable push notifications

Submit a request

Complete the form below. It opens your email client with a pre-filled message to privacy@myub.online.

Complaints

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Information and Data Protection Commission (Botswana), the national supervisory authority under the Act.
