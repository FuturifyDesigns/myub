# myub

University of Botswana student companion portal by Futurify Designs.

## Privacy & data protection

MyUB is designed to align with Botswana’s **Data Protection Act, 2024 (Act No. 18 of 2024)**.

Public legal pages:

- [Terms of Service](terms.html)
