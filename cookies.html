Cookies & similar technologies

Cookie Policy

Effective date: 21 July 2026 · Last updated: 21 July 2026

MyUB uses cookies and similar technologies (including localStorage and sessionStorage). Essential technologies keep the site secure and working. Optional preferences are used only with your choice. Manage choices anytime via the cookie banner or the button below.

1. What are cookies and similar technologies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device. MyUB also uses browser storage (localStorage / sessionStorage) for authentication state, preferences, and security. Where these technologies can identify you or be linked to you, they are treated as personal data processing under Botswana’s Data Protection Act, 2024.

2. Categories we use

2.1 Strictly necessary (always on)

  • Authentication / session storage (Supabase auth tokens)
  • CSRF / security helpers (myub_csrf_token)
  • Session idle timeout tracking (myub_last_activity)
  • Consent choice record itself (myub_consent_v1)

These are required to provide the service securely and do not require opt-in consent.

2.2 Preferences (optional)

  • Theme / dark mode preference
  • Remember-me preference flags and remembered email (if you choose Remember me)
  • UI preferences (welcome seen, banner dismissals, onboarding tour progress, GPA UI state)
  • Notification / sound preference flags stored locally

2.3 Functional / communications (optional)

  • Push notification subscription data via OneSignal (only after you enable notifications)
  • Related functional storage needed to deliver those alerts

2.4 Analytics / advertising

We do not currently use third-party advertising cookies or analytics platforms such as Google Analytics.

3. Third-party technologies

Loading fonts and libraries from CDNs (for example Google Fonts, jsDelivr, cdnjs, unpkg) may cause those providers to receive technical data such as your IP address. Push providers process device/subscription data if you opt in.

4. How to control cookies

  • Use our on-site consent banner / “Manage cookie preferences”
  • Clear site data in your browser settings
  • Disable push notifications in browser / OS settings

Blocking strictly necessary storage may prevent login or break core features.

5. More information

For broader personal-data practices, see our Privacy Policy. To exercise data rights, visit Your Rights.
