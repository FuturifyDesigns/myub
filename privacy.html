Privacy Policy

Effective date: 21 July 2026 · Last updated: 21 July 2026

In short: MyUB processes University of Botswana student personal data to provide the portal (account, academic tools, messaging, events, and related features). We do this under Botswana’s Data Protection Act, 2024 (Act No. 18 of 2024). You have rights to access, correct, erase, restrict, object, and port your data. Contact us at privacy@myub.online.

1. Who we are (Data Controller)

MyUB is operated by Futurify Designs (“we”, “us”, “our”). For personal data processed through MyUB, Futurify Designs is the data controller under the Data Protection Act, 2024.

  • Website: https://www.myub.online
  • Privacy / data requests: privacy@myub.online
  • Supervisory authority: Information and Data Protection Commission (Botswana)

2. Scope

This policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect personal data when you visit or use MyUB, including related push notifications and file uploads. It applies to students and other authorised users of the service.

3. Personal data we collect

3.1 Account and identity

  • First name, last name, full name
  • UB student ID and UB email address (@ub.ac.bw)
  • Year of study, programme type, programme name
  • Password (stored hashed by our authentication provider; we do not store plaintext passwords on our servers)
  • Profile photo / avatar (if you upload one)
  • Optional bio and GPA goal

3.2 Academic and study content

  • Courses, grades, credits, semesters, and related academic records you enter
  • Schedule / calendar events you create
  • Notes, past papers, and other files you upload

3.3 Social and communication

  • Direct messages and study-group messages
  • Friendships, friend requests, blocks, and reports
  • Event listings and RSVPs
  • Voice/video call signalling metadata when you use calls (audio/video is processed in your browser session)
  • In-app notification records

3.4 Technical and usage data

  • Online status / last seen (for presence features)
  • Connection / capacity heartbeats while you use the app
  • Device and browser information needed to operate the service
  • Local storage / session storage keys for authentication, preferences, security, and session timeout
  • Push subscription identifiers if you enable notifications

We do not currently use third-party advertising or behavioural analytics pixels (such as Google Analytics).

4. Lawful bases for processing

Under section 26 of the Data Protection Act, 2024, we process personal data only where a lawful basis applies, including:

  • Contract / service performance: creating and operating your MyUB account, academic tools, messaging, events, and core portal features you request.
  • Consent: optional features such as non-essential cookies/storage, push notifications (where consent is required), and other processing you opt into. You may withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior lawful processing.
  • Legitimate interests: securing the service, preventing abuse, capacity management, and improving reliability — balanced against your rights.
  • Legal obligation: where we must comply with applicable Botswana law or respond to lawful authority requests.

Where processing is not necessary for the service, we will not treat “forced” consent as valid.

5. How we use your data

  • Authenticate you and manage your account
  • Provide GPA, schedule, notes, past papers, events, friends, messages, and study groups
  • Show online status and deliver notifications (in-app and, if enabled, push)
  • Detect banned accounts, abuse, and security threats
  • Operate capacity limits and keep the service available
  • Respond to data-subject requests and support queries
  • Meet legal and regulatory requirements

6. Sharing and recipients (processors)

We use trusted service providers that process data on our instructions:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, realtime, and avatar storage
  • Cloudflare — hosting-related services, Workers for uploads and push relay, CDN
  • OneSignal — push notification delivery (only if you enable notifications)
  • Google Fonts / jsDelivr / cdnjs / unpkg — fonts and front-end libraries (may receive technical connection data such as IP address)
  • PeerJS / WebRTC-related libraries — call signalling when you use voice/video

We do not sell your personal data. We may disclose data if required by law, to protect users’ safety, or to investigate abuse.

7. Cross-border transfers

Some processors listed above may store or process data outside Botswana. Where personal data is transferred internationally, we take steps consistent with the Data Protection Act, 2024 — including using reputable providers, contractual protections where available, and limiting data to what is needed for the service. By creating an account and using MyUB, you acknowledge that such transfers may occur as described in this policy.

8. Retention

We keep personal data only as long as needed for the purposes above:

  • Account data: for the life of your account, then deleted or anonymised within a reasonable period after deletion
  • Messages, notes, uploads, academic records: while your account is active, unless you delete them earlier or request erasure
  • Security / capacity logs: short periods needed for operation and abuse prevention
  • Reports / moderation records: as needed to investigate and protect the community

Exact periods may vary; you can request details or erasure via Your Rights.

9. Security

We implement technical and organisational measures appropriate to the risk, including encrypted transport (HTTPS), authentication controls, client-side security helpers, access controls, and provider security features. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; please use a strong unique password and keep your device secure.

10. Children’s data

MyUB is intended for University of Botswana students. If you are under 18, you should only use MyUB with appropriate parental/guardian awareness where required. We do not knowingly market to children. If you believe a child has provided data unlawfully, contact privacy@myub.online.

11. Your rights under the Data Protection Act, 2024

Subject to legal limits, you may:

  • Be informed about how your data is processed
  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request erasure (“right to be forgotten”) in certain cases
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Object to certain processing
  • Receive data portability in a commonly used format
  • Not be subject to decisions based solely on automated processing that produce legal or similarly significant effects (MyUB does not make such solely automated decisions about you)
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information and Data Protection Commission

See Your Rights for how to exercise these rights. We aim to respond within the timeframes required by law.

12. Cookies and similar technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies (including localStorage / sessionStorage). Essential technologies are required for login and security. Non-essential preferences require your choice via our consent banner. Details: Cookie Policy.

13. Push notifications

If you enable browser / push notifications, we may send message alerts and similar notices via OneSignal. Notification content may include limited personal data (for example, a sender name and short preview). You can disable notifications in your browser or device settings at any time.

14. Account deletion

You can request deletion from your Profile page or by emailing privacy@myub.online. We will delete or anonymise personal data we control, subject to legal retention needs (for example, unresolved abuse reports).

15. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect legal or product changes. Material updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date. Continued use after changes means you acknowledge the updated policy.

16. Contact

Questions or data-protection requests:

  • Email: privacy@myub.online
  • Rights portal: data-rights.html
